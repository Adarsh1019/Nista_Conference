Tracks

Track 1

Track 1: From Data to Creation: How Industrial IoT and Generative AI are Shaping Industry 4.0 This track explores the synergy between Industrial IoT and Generative AI, focusing on their transformative impact on Industry 4.0. Topics include:  AI-driven generative design for manufacturing.  Industrial IoT data as the foundation for creative AI applications.  Real-world examples of smart design and product creation.

Track 2

Track 2: Industrial IoT for Predictive Analytics: Unlocking Efficiency through Data Dive into the power of predictive analytics driven by IoT data to enhance operational efficiency. Key areas include:  Predictive maintenance and minimizing downtime.  Real-time analytics in production lines.  Case studies of predictive models revolutionizing industry processes.

Track 3

Track 3: Smart Factory 4.0: IoT at the Heart of Digital Transformation This track emphasizes the pivotal role of IoT in driving digital transformation in smart factories. Discussions will cover:  IoT-enabled manufacturing automation.  Digital twins for enhanced production and quality control.  The integration of IoT with other Industry 4.0 technologies like AI and blockchain.

Track 4

Track 4: Cybersecurity in IoT: Protecting the Industrial Digital Backbone With the rise of connected industrial systems, cybersecurity is a critical concern. This track addresses:  Threat landscapes and vulnerabilities in Industrial IoT.  Securing industrial networks and device Strategies for robust data protection and compliance in IoT environments.

Track 5

Track 5: Industrial IoT for Sustainability: Creating Smarter, Greener Operations Explore how IoT solutions are driving sustainability and green initiatives in industries. Topics include:  Energy-efficient IoT systems and resource optimization.  IoT in circular economy practices.  Real-world examples of IoT reducing carbon footprints in industrial settings.

Track 6

Track 6: Collaborative Robots (Cobots) & IoT: The Future of Human-Machine Interaction Focusing on the collaboration between humans and intelligent machines, this track covers:  IoT-enabled cobots for industrial tasks.  Enhancing worker productivity and safety with IoT and robotics.  Future trends in IoT-driven human-machine interaction.

NISTA: Next-gen Industrial Systems Through IoT, Analytics, and AI.
